Spendly — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-07-02
Developer: Ayoub
Contact: ayoubcherqa2020@gmail.com

Summary: Spendly collects NO data. Everything stays on your device. The app has no internet access.

Data Collection

Spendly collects nothing. There is no analytics, no advertising, no user accounts, and no third-party SDKs that transmit data of any kind. Nothing you enter ever leaves your device.

Data Storage

Spendly stores your expense entries locally on your device, in the app's private storage area:

  • Your expense entries: the amount, category, and timestamp of each expense you log (for example, "$12.50, Food, today at 2:04 PM").

That's it: there are no other records, files, or caches kept by the app.

This data is:

  • Stored only on your device (never uploaded, synced, or backed up to any server operated by us).
  • Deletable at any time: remove a single expense with the delete button next to it in the Insights list, or remove everything at once by uninstalling the app.

Spendly is not a bank app: it never links to a bank account or card, and all entries are typed in manually by you.

Permissions

Spendly requests no Android permissions. It does not have access to your contacts, location, camera, microphone, storage, or the internet.

On-Device Processing

All calculations Spendly performs (daily totals, monthly totals, and category breakdowns) happen entirely on your device using the data you've entered. Nothing is sent anywhere for processing.

Children's Privacy

Spendly is not directed at children under 13. Regardless of a user's age, the app collects no data from anyone, so there is nothing to safeguard beyond what is described above.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at the same URL where you found this one.
