DocLite — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-07-02
Developer: Ayoub · Contact: ayoubcherqa2020@gmail.com

Summary: DocLite collects NO data. Everything stays on your device. The app has no internet access — it does not even hold the Android permission required to reach the network.

Data Collection

DocLite collects nothing. There are no analytics, no ads, no accounts, and no crash-reporting services. The only third-party component is Google Play Billing, used solely for the optional Pro purchase (see Purchases below) — it talks to the Google Play app on your device, not to any server of ours (we run none).

Data Storage

The documents you scan — page images and the PDFs you export — are stored only on your device, in the app's private storage. Document titles and page order are kept in a local database on your device. Nothing is uploaded anywhere, ever.

You can delete any page or document inside the app at any time; deleting removes the underlying image files too. Uninstalling the app removes everything. DocLite sets allowBackup=false, so your scans are also excluded from device backups.

When you share an exported PDF, it goes only to the app you choose in the Android share sheet; DocLite itself never sends it anywhere.

Permissions

  • Camera — used solely to photograph documents you choose to scan. Images are processed entirely on your device. DocLite does not record video or audio.

DocLite holds no other permissions: no internet, no network state, no storage, no location, no contacts. (The Google Play billing permission is present solely to enable the optional Pro purchase through the Play Store app.)

On-Device Processing

Cropping, perspective correction, filters, and PDF creation all run locally using Android's built-in graphics — no cloud services, no machine-learning uploads.

Purchases

DocLite offers an optional one-time "Pro" purchase that removes the free page limit. The purchase is processed entirely by Google Play; DocLite never sees, transmits, or stores your payment details. The app receives only an unlock confirmation from Google Play and stores that yes/no state on your device. Google's handling of payment data is described in Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

DocLite is not directed at children under 13 and collects no data from anyone, of any age.

Changes

Any future changes to this policy will be posted at this same location.
